Eagle Island Micro Triathlon

When: July 10, 2021

Course: Starting at Eagle Island (end of dirt road at the east end of the park), the course consists of a run, swim (float), bike loop, ending at the far west parking lot of Eagle Island:

  • 3/4 mile run to the South Channel of Boise River
  • 1/2 mile river float (life preservers required) to Grandma's house
  • 3.5 mile bike ride on friendly streets back to Eagle Island

Important:All contestants will start each leg of the micro-triathlon together, and wait for everyone to finish.
